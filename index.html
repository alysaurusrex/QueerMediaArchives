WELCOME!

A soon-to-be complete archive of queer media.

Our general definition of "queer media" for the application of this site is:

Any form of physical or digital media, such as films, books, and webseries, with at least one protagonist or creater that identifies as queer.

We believe that representation matters. We organized this archive to make queer media easier to find and curate. Each entry is tagged with genres and trigger warnings to filter your search.
